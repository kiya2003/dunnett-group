Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dunnett Group Consulting Ltd collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit our website or get in touch with us. We are committed to handling your information responsibly and in line with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Dunnett Group is the trading name of Dunnett Group Consulting Ltd, a company registered in England & Wales (Company No. 17153583). Our registered office is [registered office address to be confirmed]. For the purposes of data protection law, we are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at info@dunnettgroup.com or on +44 (0)7947 678562.

What information we collect

We only collect what we need to respond to you and provide our services:

  • Information you give us: when you complete our enquiry form or email us, this may include your name, email address, company name, phone number (if you provide it) and the content of your message.
  • Technical information: when you visit the site, our hosting provider may automatically log limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type and the time of access, for security and performance purposes.

How we use your information and our lawful basis

We use your personal data to:

  • respond to your enquiry and arrange a discovery call;
  • provide, and where relevant invoice for, our services;
  • keep records for our legitimate business purposes; and
  • maintain the security and performance of our website.

Our lawful bases for processing are your consent (where you choose to contact us), our legitimate interests in responding to enquiries and running our business, and, where we agree to provide services, the performance of a contract.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, our website hosting and email providers), who process data on our behalf under appropriate agreements. Our website displays fonts served by Google Fonts; when the site loads, your browser requests these from Google's servers, which may process your IP address. We may also disclose information where required by law.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and client information only for as long as necessary to respond to you, deliver our services and meet our legal and accounting obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access your personal data, to have it corrected or erased, to restrict or object to our processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please email info@dunnettgroup.com.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113.

Cookies

Information about the cookies used on this site is set out in our Cookie Policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
