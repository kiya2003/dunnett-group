About Dunnett Group

A passion for practical EHS solutions

At Dunnett Group we provide professional, structured Environmental, Health & Safety support, delivered flexibly to suit your business. We understand that no two organisations face the same risks or regulatory pressure, which is why our services are designed to scale, from simple compliance support through to a fully embedded EHS partnership.

Our approach is experienced and practical: real-world advice that protects your people and your reputation, not paperwork for the sake of it.

  • Trusted across petrochemical, construction, marine and SME sectors
  • Full regulatory compliance, COMAH, COSHH, RAMS and more
  • From one-off projects to ongoing EHS leadership
  • Clear, jargon-free guidance you can act on
What We Do

Comprehensive EHS expertise

From first steps toward compliance to the highest safety standards, we support organisations of every size.

EHS Management Systems

From full implementation to reviewing existing systems, gap analysis, EHS strategy & risk planning, project scoping and feasibility.

Safe Systems of Work

Policies, procedures and written instructions, risk assessments and method statements, CDM and environmental impact assessments.

EHS Project Management

On-site management, contractor safety coordination, permit-to-work systems, hazard identification & mitigation, safety meetings and toolbox talks.

Auditing & Accreditation

Internal and external audits, ISO and ESG support, CHAS and accreditation preparation, post-incident review.

Training

Safety leadership training, compliance-based and risk assessment courses, emergency response and First Aid at Work.

EHS Consultancy

Expert regulatory advice covering UK and EU regulations, plus thorough incident investigation support.
