Our Services

What we deliver

A comprehensive suite of professional services, caring for a diverse clientele - from small teams taking their first steps toward compliance to established enterprises striving for the highest safety standards.

EHS Management Systems

Full implementation through to reviewing existing management systems: EHS gap analysis, EHS strategy & risk planning, project scoping and feasibility support.

Safe Systems of Work

Policies, procedures and written instructions, risk assessments and method statements (RAMS), CDM and environmental impact assessments.

EHS Project Management

On-site EHS project management, contractor safety coordination, permit-to-work systems and control, hazard identification & mitigation planning, safety meetings and toolbox talks.

Auditing & Accreditation

Internal and external audits, ISO and ESG support, CHAS and accreditation preparation, and post-incident review.

Training

Safety leadership training, compliance-based and risk assessment training, emergency response and First Aid at Work.

EHS Consultancy

Expert regulatory advice covering UK and EU regulations, with thorough incident investigation support.

  • Risk assessment review
  • Annual compliance check
  • Email support (fair use)

Most popular Silver Active Compliance Support
