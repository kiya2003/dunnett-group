Find the right level of support

Every engagement is tailored following a short discovery discussion, so you only pay for the support you actually need.

Bronze Compliance Foundation

Ideal for sole traders, start-ups and lower-risk operations.

  • Health & safety policy support
  • Risk assessment review
  • Annual compliance check
  • Email support (fair use)

A cost-effective way to establish legal compliance and peace of mind.

Most popular Silver Active Compliance Support

Suitable for SMEs, contractors, education settings and growing businesses.

  • Policy, RAMS and COSHH support
  • Scheduled site visits / inspections
  • Incident advice & investigation support
  • Phone and email advisory access

The level most clients start at.

Gold Embedded EHS Partner

For higher-risk activities, multi-site operations or organisations needing ongoing EHS leadership.

  • Monthly site visits / inspections
  • Ongoing RAMS, COSHH & risk register ownership
  • Training sessions & toolbox talks
  • Audit readiness & regulator liaison
  • Strategic EHS planning

Full EHS leadership, embedded in your operation.

We also provide one-off and project support, compliance gap analysis, internal audits, RAMS & COSHH development, training delivery, incident investigation, and CHAS / ISO / accreditation support.
